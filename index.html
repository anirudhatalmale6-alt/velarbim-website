What We Do

Our Services

We provide end-to-end BIM services for the MEP disciplines. Whether you need detailed modelling, coordination support, or strategic BIM consulting, we bring the technical depth and project experience to deliver.

01

MEP BIM Modelling

We create detailed, construction-ready 3D models for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems using Autodesk Revit. Our models are built to LOD 300-400, fully parametric, and coordinated with architectural and structural disciplines.

  • HVAC ductwork and pipework modelling
  • Electrical containment and distribution layouts
  • Plumbing and drainage systems
  • Equipment scheduling and specification
  • Model auditing and quality checks
02

Clash Detection & Coordination

Spatial conflicts between building systems are inevitable on complex projects. We run comprehensive clash detection using Navisworks and Revit, identifying and resolving issues before they reach the construction site.

  • Multi-discipline clash detection (MEP vs Structure vs Architecture)
  • Clash resolution and coordination meetings
  • 4D sequencing support
  • Regular coordination model updates
  • Clash reports and resolution tracking
03

BIM Consulting & Management

For firms looking to adopt or improve their BIM processes, we provide strategic consulting. From BIM Execution Plans to software training, we help teams work smarter.

  • BIM Execution Plan development
  • Standards and template creation
  • Software selection and setup
  • Team training and upskilling
  • Project BIM management
04

Virtual Design & Construction (VDC)

We bridge the gap between design models and construction reality. Our VDC services help project teams visualise, plan, and coordinate complex builds before breaking ground.

  • Construction sequence visualisation
  • Prefabrication coordination
  • Site logistics planning
  • Progress tracking against BIM models

Who We Are

About VelarBIM

VelarBIM is a specialist BIM consultancy focused on the MEP disciplines. Founded by Ahmad, we bring over a decade of hands-on engineering experience to every project.

We work with contractors, developers, and design firms across Australia and the UK, providing the technical expertise needed to deliver complex building services on time and without surprises.

Ahmad

MEP BIM/VDC Mechanical Engineer & Founder

Ahmad is an MEP BIM/VDC Mechanical Engineer with more than 10 years of experience delivering BIM solutions on major construction projects. His portfolio spans hospitals, data centres, commercial towers, airports, and educational facilities across Sydney, London, and regional centres in both countries. He combines deep technical knowledge of MEP systems with practical construction experience, ensuring models are not just accurate but buildable.

What We Do

Our Services

We provide end-to-end BIM services for the MEP disciplines. Whether you need detailed modelling, coordination support, or strategic BIM consulting, we bring the technical expertise and industry knowledge to deliver results.

01

MEP BIM Modelling

We create detailed, construction-ready 3D models for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems using Autodesk Revit. Our models are built to LOD 300-400, fully parametric, and coordinated for seamless integration with architectural and structural models.

  • HVAC ductwork and pipework modelling
  • Electrical containment and distribution layouts
  • Plumbing and drainage systems
  • Equipment scheduling and specification
  • Model auditing and quality checks
02

Clash Detection & Coordination

Identify and resolve conflicts before construction. We perform comprehensive clash detection and coordination reviews to eliminate costly on-site clashes and ensure smooth construction delivery.

  • 3D clash detection and reporting
  • MEP to structural and architectural coordination
  • Trade coordination meetings and facilitation
  • Clash resolution strategies and documentation
  • Pre-construction coordination
03

BIM Consulting & Strategy

Develop BIM strategies tailored to your project requirements. We provide guidance on model development, information standards, and best practices to maximize the value of BIM throughout your project lifecycle.

  • BIM strategy and planning
  • Information requirements definition
  • Model standards and protocols
  • Team training and support
  • Process optimization
04

As-Built & Record Models

Create accurate as-built and record BIM models that reflect the constructed reality. These become invaluable handover documents and operational references for maintenance and future renovations.

  • As-built survey and verification
  • Record model development
  • Laser scanning and point cloud processing
  • O&M manual support and integration
  • Handover documentation
05

Construction Drawings

Extract intelligent, annotation-rich construction drawings directly from BIM models. Ensure consistency, accuracy, and compliance with your design and regulatory standards.

  • System isometric drawings
  • Plan, section, and detail drawings
  • Equipment schedules and specifications
  • Shop drawing preparation
06

Fire Protection Drawings

We produce specialist fire protection system drawings to support compliance and construction. Detailed layouts covering all aspects of fire safety services.

  • Sprinkler system layouts and risers
  • Smoke extraction and ventilation systems
  • Fire-rated service penetration details
  • Fire alarm and detection layouts
  • Compliance documentation support
07

Specifications & Tender Support

Develop comprehensive MEP specifications and tender documents extracted from BIM models. Streamline procurement with clear, detailed, and standardized documentation.

  • System specifications and schedules
  • Tender document preparation
  • Equipment selection and specification
  • Compliance requirements documentation
  • Performance standards definition
